Hi! I am Anirudh.
i develop software solutions for network operators to ensure they can describe their *intent* when making changes in the network. This intent is through code -- such as Python code, YAML/JSON constructs, or Ansible playbooks. I have dedicated to doing this well since 2020 as a network automation engineer at [Baxter](https://baxter.com).

### Personal statement

I am exploring about how network operators and application deployment engineers can describe **intent** at every touchpoint in the network.

#### Why?

After having worked in the network automation space, I picked up an interest in **network programmability** and discovered the world of dataplane programmability (P4, NPLang), SmartNICs, Linux networking programmability (think XDP, DPDK), and everything related to *programming network elements*. I am excited about this field and the impact it has on how we design networks and clouds for modern applications and internet/mobility services.
